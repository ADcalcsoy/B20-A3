Midterm Instructions

Mid term exam for B20 is on February 28th during class time (9 to 11) in HL B101 (where we meet for lectures).
The exam is based on database portion we covered in class. That includes, Relational Algebra, and SQL queries.
The exam will not include any web application content we covered last week.
You can prepare for this exam by reviewing all the queries we have learned so far in lectures, tutorials and assignment.

Exam format:

  • 2 Short questions
    • Multiple choice questions or brief answers
  • 5 queries based on RA.
  • 5 queries based on SQL.

You need to bring pen/pencil to write your exam.

General rules for exam:

  • It is closed book exam.
  • no aids of any kind allowed(calculator, notes, textbooks, online resources, tablets, people).
  • Phones should be switched off during exam.
  • You have 2 hours to write exam.
